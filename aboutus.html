ABOUT US


At Golden Dragon, we bring the authentic taste of Chinese cuisine to your table. Since our opening, we have been a meeting point for lovers of oriental food, offering a unique culinary experience that combines tradition and creativity.

Our menu is carefully prepared by expert chefs, who use fresh, high-quality ingredients to prepare each dish. From classics like sweet and sour chicken and fried rice to regional specialties like Peking duck and dim sum, every bite will transport you to the colorful streets of China.

We pride ourselves on not only serving delicious food, but also providing a welcoming and authentic atmosphere. Our restaurant is decorated with traditional Chinese elements that create a warm and welcoming atmosphere, ideal for enjoying a meal with family and friends.

Furthermore, at Golden Dragon we are committed to excellence in service. Our friendly and attentive staff is always ready to make your visit an unforgettable experience. We invite you to discover the rich flavors and aromas of Chinese cuisine in our restaurant.
